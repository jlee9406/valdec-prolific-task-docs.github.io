Participant Information & Consent

Welcome to our economic decision-making study!

In this study, we investigate how people learn to make reward-based decisions. You will complete a task in which you choose between two images that differ in their reward probability. Your goal is to discover which image has the higher reward probability and pick it as often as possible.

Can I participate?
To be eligible, you must meet all of the following criteria:
  • 18 years of age or older
  • Fluent in English
  • Have not previously participated in this experiment

How much can I earn?
You will be paid 9 GBP for completing the study. On top of this, you can earn up to 3 GBP bonus based on your performance — the more rewards you collect, the higher your bonus.

How long does it take?
The study will take roughly 50–60 minutes. You can take one break during the task.

What device should I use?
Please use a computer, laptop, or tablet. A phone screen is too small for this task.

Confidentiality
Your participation will remain confidential. Your Prolific ID will not be shared with anyone outside the research team.

Participation and withdrawal
Your participation is completely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time; however, you will only be paid upon completion and after returning to Prolific using the link provided at the end of the study.

Contact
If you have questions about your participation or payment, please contact the team.

Data Protection
Your data will be stored securely and can be requested through the contact above. By accepting this agreement, you consent that your data may be used within the lab and shared or published in anonymised form.
